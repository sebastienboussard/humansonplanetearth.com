How It Works

1

Monthly Word

Each month, we announce a new word that serves as the theme for all essay submissions.

2

Submit Essays

Writers have until the 25th to submit their essays exploring the monthly word (max 1 page).

3

Community Voting

From the 26th-30th, our community votes for the most compelling and thought-provoking essay.

4

Winner Announced

The winning essay is featured in our Winners Gallery and the author receives recognition.
